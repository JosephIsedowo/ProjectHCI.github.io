Tutorial

In this study, your task is to get one or more scrolling techniques to land either the row of stars or a specific line precisely within the target grey area situated at the center of the webpage.

Your objective is to get the row of stars or the specific line, as indicated in the black banner at the top of the page, into the designated grey area. It's crucial to halt your scrolling when the target is fully contained within the grey area, rather than merely scrolling past it. Both the location of the stars row and the target line numbers will vary, and the dimensions of the grey area will also change.
